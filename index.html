IPD App — How To Use
Follow these steps in order. Take your time — once you know it, it's quick.

The IPD app lives in your phone's browser — it is not downloaded from the App Store. It works like a website but behaves like an app.

💡 Add it to your home screen so you can open it with one tap, just like any other app.
How to add to home screen (iPhone):
  1. Open Safari and go to the app link your manager gave you
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add — the IPD icon will appear on your home screen
How to add to home screen (Android):
  1. Open Chrome and go to the app link
  2. Tap the three dots menu (top right)
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  4. Tap Add

When you open the app you'll see the Home Screen. This is your starting point for everything.

🔧
New Quote — Start a quote for a job. Use this for pricing up work for a customer.
📦
Stock Order — Build a list of materials you need to order from Reece. Prices come through automatically.
🎓

💬

💡 The bottom bar has navigation buttons — Quote, Labour, Review, and more. These appear once you're in a job.

Before you add anything to a quote, you need to enter the SM8 job number. This links your quote to the correct job in ServiceM8.

How to do it:
  1. Tap New Quote on the home screen
  2. At the top of the screen you'll see a Job Number field
  3. Tap it and type in the job number — you'll find this in ServiceM8 (e.g. 7282)
⚠️ Always enter the job number first before adding items. If you forget, the quote won't link to the right job in SM8.

The app searches the full Reece product catalogue — over 42,000 products — directly from your phone.

How to search:
  1. Make sure the Reece tab is selected (not Bundles)
  2. Type what you're looking for in the search bar — e.g. "15mm comp tee" or "isolation valve"
  3. Results appear below — scroll through to find the right product
  4. Tap the + button next to the item to add it to your quote
  5. Tap + again to increase the quantity, or use the − button to reduce it
💡 Search tips: Use the size and type — "20mm ball valve" works better than just "valve". The more specific you are, the better the results.
💡 You can also tap the 🎤 microphone button to search by voice — just say the product name out loud.

Bundles are pre-built material lists for common jobs — like a hot water unit changeout or a new bathroom fit-off. Instead of searching for each item one by one, you add the whole bundle at once.

How to use a bundle:
  1. Tap the Bundles tab at the top of the search screen
  2. Type the job type — e.g. "hot water" or "basin"
  3. Find the bundle you want and tap it
  4. All items from the bundle are added to your quote automatically
  5. You can then adjust quantities or add extra items from Reece
💡 Bundles save a lot of time on repeat jobs. Ask your supervisor which bundles are set up for common IPD jobs.

After adding your materials, you need to add the labour charge. This covers the time spent on the job.

How to add labour:
  1. Tap the Labour button on the bottom navigation bar
  2. Select the correct rate type:
Standard$229/hrNormal tradie rate
2nd Tradesman$160/hrSecond tradie on same job
Builder Rate$195/hrFor builder clients
Apprentice$100/hrApprentice labour
Service Fee$88 flatCall-out / service charge
  1. Enter the number of hours (e.g. 2.5 for two and a half hours)
  2. The labour line is added to your quote automatically
⚠️ If you're unsure which rate to use, ask your supervisor before sending the quote.

Before sending anything to SM8, always review your quote to make sure everything is correct.

How to review:
  1. Tap the Review button on the bottom navigation bar
  2. Check each item — name, quantity, and price
  3. Check the labour lines are correct
  4. Check the job number at the top is correct
  5. If anything is wrong, go back and fix it before sending
💡 It's much easier to fix a mistake here than after it's been sent to SM8.

Once you're happy with everything, send the quote to SM8. It will appear on the job automatically — no typing required.

How to send:
  1. Make sure the job number is entered correctly
  2. Tap the Send to ServiceM8 button
  3. Wait for the green confirmation message — "Quote sent to ServiceM8!"
  4. Open SM8 and check the job — all your items should be there with prices
⚠️ If you get an error message, check the job number is correct and try again. If it still fails, contact the office.
💡 After sending, the quote resets so you're ready for the next job.

A stock order is used when you need to order materials from Reece — not for quoting a customer, just for getting your materials sorted.

How to do a stock order:
  1. Tap Stock Order on the home screen
  2. Search for each product you need using the Reece search tab
  3. Tap + to add items and set quantities
  4. You'll see the cost price (what IPD pays Reece) — not the retail price
  5. Once you have everything, tap Send to ServiceM8
  6. The order will appear in SM8 for the office to process
💡 Stock orders use cost prices — this is what helps the office track spending. Don't worry about markup on stock orders.

The app has a dark mode for working at night or in low light conditions.

How to toggle dark mode:
  1. Look for the 🌙 moon icon on the home screen or top bar
  2. Tap it to switch between dark and light mode
  3. The setting is saved — it will remember your preference next time

The AI Assistant is a built-in tool you can ask plumbing questions, compliance questions, or get help with anything job-related.

How to use it:
  1. Tap the AI Assistant (✨) icon on the home screen
  2. Type your question — e.g. "What is the minimum grade for a DN100 drain?"
  3. Read the answer — it draws on Australian plumbing standards
💡 Good for quick compliance checks on site. Always double-check critical decisions with your supervisor or the full standard.
❌ Quote sent but nothing appears in SM8
Check the job number is correct. Open SM8 and look at the job — sometimes it takes 30 seconds to appear. If still nothing, try sending again.
❌ Can't find a product in the search
Try different words — e.g. "compression" instead of "comp", or just the size like "15mm tee". If still not found, add it manually in SM8 after sending.
❌ Prices showing as $0.00
This can happen with some product codes. Send the quote anyway and open the job in SM8 — tap the green Reece button next to the item to pull the price through manually.
❌ App not loading or showing a blank screen
Close the app completely and reopen it. If on poor signal, wait until you have better reception. The app needs internet to search products and send to SM8.
❌ Sent to wrong job number
Open SM8, find the wrong job, and delete the items. Then redo the quote with the correct job number. Let your supervisor know.
Questions? Contact the office via WhatsApp or call Matt.
